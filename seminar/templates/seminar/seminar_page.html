About The Event

The Ministry of Transport and Communications (MTC) of Timor-Leste serves as the principal authority responsible for formulating, implementing, and overseeing national policies in the transport and communications sectors. In the communications sector, the MTC is at the forefront of the country's digital transformation, driving the development of modern infrastructure and promoting digital inclusion. Through strategic policies and initiatives, the ministry aims to enhance connectivity, improve public service delivery, and stimulate economic growth by fostering a robust digital ecosystem aligned with international standards and best practices.
